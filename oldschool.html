The Sisters of the Flame are a group of conjurers dedicated to Old School Magic: The Gathering in New York City. We adhere to the Eternal Central B&R and set legality but are also currently experimenting with the new Atlantic rules.

You can find us on Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.


We also maintain this Google Map listing of all the Old School maps in the world. If you have a new group or more information to add, contact me on FB (Paul DeSilva) or on the Discord or Instagram (8bit_mtg).
